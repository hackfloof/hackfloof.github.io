For grant seekers

Amanaska works as a consultancy, introducing tailored shortlists of funding opportunities to potential donors.

We are always on the lookout for the right new projects to add to our portfolio but are not ourselves a grant giving body; Our remit is defined by the interests and priorites of the donors with whom we work and the final decision on which proposals are successful is entirely down to them.

General guidelines

We will not be able to help with general fundraising appeals and are at present unable to respond to approaches from any of the following:

  • private individuals
  • Any organisation without the relevant acreditation to demonstate not for profit status as governed by the relevant jurisdictions in whichever country it is based.
  • Any organisation with fewer than 2 years audited accounts and a proven track record in successful program implementation.
  • political pressure groups, advocacy groups
  • religious organisations
  • animal welfare organisations or medical research bodies
